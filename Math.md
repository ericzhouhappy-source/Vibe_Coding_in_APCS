## $e^{iπ}$
$e^{iπ} = con(π)+isin(π) = -1 = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{(i\pi)^n}{n!} = 1 + i\pi - \frac{\pi^2}{2!} - \frac{i\pi^3}{3!} + \frac{\pi^4}{4!} + \dots $
